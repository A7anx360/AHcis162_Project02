Parfait

The lovable & goofy Poodle. As Lucy & Alice's pet dog, he is very loyal & likes to play fetch. He always obeys commands, except when he sees a squirrel or another dog.

Weight Class: Middle
Favorite Vehicle: Squeaky Red Cruiser
Guero

The terrible Terrier of terror. Guero is Parfait's insane cousin, he doesn't like fetch & he hates everyone. He also lives alone in a cave.

Weight Class: Middle
Favorite Vehicle: Rusty ol'Rag
Neige

The giant Rabbit of Flanders. Neige is a bear-sized rabbit owned by a friend of Lucy's. He is not only big & strong, but he's also cunning & pretty mean sometimes. He also strongly dislikes Parfait.

Weight Class: Heavy
Favorite Vehicle: Carrocket Scooter
Guinea

The cowardly Pig. This Guinea pig may be skittish but he has no respect for creatures larger than him. It doesn't matter how friendly you are to him, he will bite you. He especially doesn't like Parfait. He is the pet of a boy named Timothy, who has a crush on Lucy.

Weight Class: Light
Favorite Vehicle: Tiny Destroyer of All Worlds
Luna

The adorable Dwarf-Hamster. As Lucy's clingy pet hamster, she often likes to beg for snacks. Among other things, Luna can sometimes be found sleeping inside one of Lucy's pockets. Luna is also best friends with Mimi the fairy & a Hamster named Remy.

Weight Class: Feather
Favorite Vehicle: Hamster Ball Bike
Squinky

The rabbit genius of Holland. Despite her Goofy disposition, Squinky is actually a genius. She knows how to read, she can fly upside down (by using her ears as a propeller), & she can do calculus. The only thing Squinky can't do is talk to humans. Squinky often makes Lucy & Alice laugh.

Weight Class: Light
Favorite Vehicle: Avocado Disaster
Squawk

The furiously feathered Parakeet. This Parrokeet loves to pick fights with Luna, often losing surprisingly. She likes to steal food from people's mouths, & loves to eat french fries. She is the pet of Lucy's parents.

Weight Class: Feather
Favorite Vehicle: Personally Prefers the Air
Samanthis

Samantha + Mantis, Get it? Samanthis is the pet of a fairy named Mimi, who is friends with Lucy. Samathis can often be found preying on Syntax-Moths (which are a type of glitch-bug ruining the game they live in).

Weight Class: Feather
Favorite Vehicle: Centipede Bike
